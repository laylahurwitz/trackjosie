Thailand

April 5 – 14, 2025

April 5
Sattahip → Chiang Mai

Teaching contract ends. Bus from Sattahip to Bangkok, then overnight sleeper train to Chiang Mai — arriving 7:15am on April 6.

April 6 – 8
Pa Pae Meditation Retreat

Bus directly from Chiang Mai station. Two nights of silence and stillness before the adventure kicks off.

April 8 – 10
Pai

Nolo Hub hostel (Josie booked — pay the rest in cash only on arrival). Head to Pai Authentic Travel on walking street; tell them you're at Nolo Hub and they'll call for a pickup.

  • Pai Canyon sunset
  • Gender Bender Night (Wednesdays)
  • Free hostel activities every night
  • Bars on walking street
April 10 – 11
Chiang Rai

Van Pai → Chiang Mai (10:30am–2:20pm), then Green Bus from Chiang Mai Arcade Terminal 3 (5pm–8:45pm). Busket Hostel overnight, morning temples, evening bus back.

  • White Temple (Wat Rong Khun)
  • Blue Temple (Wat Rong Suea Ten)
  • Explore downtown Chiang Rai midday
April 11 – 14
Chiang Mai — Songkran 🎉

Sherloft home hostel. Songkran water festival on April 13 — Thailand's biggest New Year celebration. Flight out April 14 at 10:30am, arriving Hanoi 6:10pm.

  • Khao Soi Maesai (best khao soi in town)
  • Nong Ho Vintage Market
  • Jing Jai vintage and food market
🇻🇳

Vietnam

April 14 – 29, 2025

⚠️ Get Vietnam visa early March — before leaving Thailand
April 14 – 16
Hanoi

AirBnB in the Old Quarter (check in 3pm — 8 Phố Lý Nam Đế Hàng Mã). Post-It Café, Hoan Kiem Lake, museums, beer corners and live music.

April 16 – 20
Ha Giang Loop 🏍

Sleeper bus Hanoi → Ha Giang night of the 16th. Four days on motorbikes through Vietnam's most dramatic mountain scenery. Return to Hanoi ~4pm on the 20th; stay at Rainbow Homestay (Số 8 ngõ 18B Tông Đản, Hoàn Kiếm).

April 21 – 22
Ha Long Bay ⛵

Overnight boat cruise. Kayaking, limestone caves, emerald water. Ferry Cat Ba → Ha Long ($3). Overnight bus to Sapa departs 9:30pm from Ha Long International Cruise Port, arriving 6am.

  • Cat Ba ferry times: 9am, 11:30am, 1pm, 3pm, 4pm
April 23 – 25
Sapa

Arrive 6am. Mao's trekking and homestay in Su Pan village. Bus to Hanoi airport April 25, fly Hanoi → Da Nang (7:45pm–8:45pm).

April 25 – 29
Hoi An ✂️

Private room at Harmony Old Town Hostel. Go to 41 Tailor at the cloth market on Day 1 — clothes take a couple of days. Old Town is magical day and night.

  • 41 Tailor inside Hoi An cloth market — Day 1
  • Madam Khanh – The Banh Mi Queen (Bourdain's pick)
  • Linh Vidal Hair Salon
  • Mina Eyelash — lash lift and tint
  • Big linen shopping haul
  • Flight to Cebu April 29
🇵🇭

Philippines

April 29 – May 8, 2025

April 29 – 30
Cebu

One night transit at Mactan Grand Golden Hotel near the airport. Early flight to Coron at 8:40am next morning.

April 30 – May 2
Coron

Hop Hostel. Arrive at Outpost Beach Hostel by 6pm on May 1 for mandatory expedition briefing. Cash only — settle 14,440 PHP (~$242 USD) before it starts.

  • Packing list: castawaytours.ph/what-to-pack
May 2 – 4
Castaway Boat Trip ⛵

Three days sailing Coron to El Nido across the Calamian Islands. Snorkeling, island stops, sleeping under stars on deck.

May 4 – 8
El Nido

Frendz Hostel (Josie booked — pay remainder on arrival). Book Haqqy Life island hopping. On May 8: bus to Puerto Princesa (7am, ~6 hours), fly PPS → Bali (1:50pm).

  • Haqqy Life island hopping tour
  • Nacpan Beach
🇮🇩

Indonesia

May 8 – 14, 2025

May 8 – 14
Ubud, Bali 🌿

Junjungan Suite villa for the week. Rice terraces, waterfalls, yoga, and spa — the perfect decompression before Australia.

  • Tegalalang & Jatiluwih rice terraces (Jatiluwih is UNESCO World Heritage)
  • Waterfalls: Sekumpul, Tukad Cepung, Leke Leke
  • Yogabarn, Alchemy, Lumeria; House of Chi for sound baths
  • Sayuri vegan, BLEND cafe, TIS Cafe, Camaya, Karti Bali spa
Day trip
Nusa Penida 🐠

Manta ray snorkel — ask operators to go to Manta Point specifically, not just Manta Bay. Check TripAdvisor for best operator.

Explore
Uluwatu 🌅

Check events at Savaya. Suluban beach, Uluwatu temple at sunset. Worth the drive down from Ubud.

If time allows
Gili Islands + Lombok

Pipes or Botchan hostel near Kuta. Trusted driver Rudi Alloevera: +62 818-0353-5335. Or Yogi Afriandi: +62 878-5093-0073.

  • Tiu Kelep waterfall
  • Bukit Merese lookout at sunset
  • Pink sand beach
  • Ashtari restaurant — free shuttle from Kuta
🇦🇺

Australia

May 14 – 31, 2025

May 14 – 19
Sydney

AirBnB in Paddington or Surry Hills. Five days to explore the city and beaches before the road trip north.

  • Henrietta Charcoal Chicken in Surry Hills (next to Yo Chi)
May 19 – 25
Byron Bay 🚐

Pick up the camper van. Probably one of the best weeks of the whole trip — slow mornings, beautiful beaches, great food and energy.

  • Rae's Hotel happy hour
  • The Roadhouse Byron Bay
  • Byron General Store
  • B Smoothie Bar
May 25 – 28
Noosa

Road trip north with beautiful stops along the way. Fairy pools, Kari Island (Rainbow Beach), kayaks. Return camper van here.

  • Road trip stops: Glass House Mountains, Australia Zoo, Mary Cairncross, Maleny, Montville, Eumundi
  • Where to eat: Mary Cairncross café, The Edge Restaurant, Sumi Open Kitchen
May 29 – 31
Whitsundays 🐠

The grand finale. Whitehaven Beach and the Great Barrier Reef. Fly home May 31.

  • Sailaway Outer Reef from Port Douglas (~$220/person) — sailawayportdouglas.com
  • Wavelength tour also recommended
